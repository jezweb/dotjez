# Save meeting recordings before they expire

Most cloud meeting tools delete recordings automatically after a set window (often around 30 days). If a call matters (a client sign-off, a workshop, a training), download it somewhere permanent that same week, or it's gone.

| If you... | Do |
|---|---|
| Finish a recorded call you'll want later | Download it to the project folder that week |
| Assume "it's in the cloud, it's safe" | Check the auto-delete window first |

## Gotchas
- The countdown starts at the recording date, not when you remember to look.
